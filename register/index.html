Refund Policy

  1. For Early/Regular registration:
    • Within 24 hours of purchase: 100% refund on registration and guest room fees
    • Within 7 days of purchase: 50% refund on registration and guest room fees
    • Beyond 7 days of purchase: No refund on any fee
  2. For Late registration:
    • You will be able to receive a full refund if you contact us within 24 hours of your purchase. Purchases are no longer eligible for refund 24 hours after the purchase.
    • Our refund policy is designed to keep registration fees affordable and help cover our prepaid expenses as a small non-profit.
    • Refunds are issued to the original payment method less a nominal administrative fee for non-refundable transaction costs.

To initiate a refund, please email delegatescanmun@gmail.com with the subject line ‘Refund Request - Delegate Name’.

Rooming Policy

Delegate registration pricing includes the cost of two nights at the Sheraton Wall Centre in quadruple occupancy rooms. For those requesting lower occupancy rooming options, this is included in the registration form. Overnight delegates must be supervised by an adult chaperone (18 years or older) who is staying overnight at the hotel. The secretariat will not hold any liabilities of what happens outside of conference hours/meeting rooms.

Day Delegates

Instead of registering as a regular delegate, attendees have the option of registering as Day Delegates. Day Delegates are able to attend all conference events including the delegate social, however they do not stay at the Sheraton Centre Toronto Hotel overnight. The cost of attending as a Day Delegate is lower to reflect the removal of an overnight hotel stay.

Independent Delegate Policy

Delegates attending the conference independently do not have to fill out the delegation/group registration form, and are only required to fill out the delegate registration form.


Independent delegates will have access to the same conference events as delegation/groups, and will not face any barriers if they do choose to attend independently.

Delegation Policy

Must have at least 1 chaperone over the age of 18 for your delegation.


Delegations must be at least 5 delegates. Delegations with over 20 delegates get a 10% discount on chaperone/head delegate room fee. Delegations with over 40 delegates get 20% off on 1 whole room.


If you wish to add or remove delegates from your delegation, please fill out the ‘DELEGATION CHANGES’ form and our secretariat will get in contact with you.
