About Us

About CANMUN

Canada Model United Nations (CANMUN) is a national-level Model United Nations (MUN) conference founded in 2022. The conference is held annually in downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada, and is open to both national and international participants from around the world.

CANMUN is a unique MUN experience that caters to both overnight and day stays, making it accessible to all participants. It is the largest overnight conference in Canada, with an expected annual attendance of 500-1000+ delegates.

As a professional MUN conference, CANMUN is dedicated to providing a high-quality experience for all attendees. The conference provides informative background guides, keynote speakers, and extensively trained staff to ensure that each moment of the conference is executed with quality.

During the conference, delegates will have the opportunity to participate in a wide range of committees whether it be a General Assembly, Specialized Committee, Crisis, or Regional Body. The conference is designed to provide a challenging and thought-provoking experience for all attendees, with an emphasis on diplomacy, leadership, and public speaking skills.

Overall, Canada Model United Nations is a professional and engaging conference that provides a platform for students to engage in meaningful discussions on global issues and to develop their leadership and diplomatic skills. With its emphasis on inclusivity and accessibility, CANMUN is a valuable opportunity for students from Canada and around the world to connect, learn, and grow.
