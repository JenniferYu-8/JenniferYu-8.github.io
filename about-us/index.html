About Us

Canada Model United Nations (CANMUN) is an annual three-day conference that brings together high school students from around the world. This prestigious event, held in Toronto, Canada, offers a unique opportunity for participants to engage in an educational simulation of the real United Nations.

With participants from countries far and wide, the inaugural CANMUN conference promises to be a truly global experience. Delegates will represent various countries in a range of international bodies, from the World Health Organization to Interpol. Committees will feature topics that are modern, historical, and fictional in nature, providing delegates with a diverse variety of issues to debate.

Attending CANMUN offers high school students the chance to develop important skills such as public speaking, self-confidence, and interpersonal communication. During committee sessions, delegates will engage in lively debate and diplomacy, working together to create innovative solutions to some of the world's most pressing issues.

Our dedicated staff team and exceptional quality of debate aim to inspire the next generation of global leaders. The secretariat team is excited to welcome all delegates, teachers, and chaperones to the beautiful city of Toronto this upcoming March. We look forward to an outstanding event and an unforgettable experience for all!
