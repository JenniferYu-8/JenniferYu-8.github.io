*Conference Collaborators

All conferences in collaboration with CANMUN will be able to display their opportunities to CANMUN attendees.


Conferences will be given spaces and tables to set up their own stands. Brochures, flyers, merchandise, discount coupons, giveaways, contests or sweepstakes and any other promotional activities of the conferenceâ€™s choice will be present!


Come interact with Conference Ambassadors for any questions!

Qualifications:

  • All delegations over 40 delegates will be given a free stand!
  • Conferences may also purchase stands for a low price of $50 from January 1st 2024 to March 31st 2024! (Priority is given to delegations over 40 first.)
  • Conferences held by schools, regions, cities, provinces/states, countries, and etc. are all permitted!
