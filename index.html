Letter from the Secretary General of CANMUN 2024

Dear Prospective Delegates, Faculty Advisors, and Staff,

It is with great excitement that I extend a warm welcome to all of you to the Canada Model United Nations (CANMUN) conference, to be held in the beautiful city of Toronto, Ontario, Canada. As Secretary General, I am honored to be leading this international conference and bringing together a diverse group of individuals from around the world to engage in meaningful dialogue, foster a sense of community, and promote the ideals of diplomacy and cooperation.

To our delegates, CANMUN offers a unique opportunity to participate in a simulation of the United Nations and represent a member state in discussions on some of the world's most pressing issues. You will have the opportunity to hone your critical thinking, negotiation, and public speaking skills, while also gaining a deeper understanding of international affairs and building lasting relationships with individuals from different cultures.

For our faculty advisors, I would like to thank you for your continued support and for the role you play in preparing our delegates for the conference. I am confident that CANMUN will provide a valuable educational experience for your students, and I encourage you to take advantage of the opportunities for professional development that will be available during the conference.

And to our secretariat and staff, I express my sincerest gratitude for your commitment to CANMUN. Your tireless efforts and dedication to creating a dynamic and enriching experience for our delegates and advisors are greatly appreciated. I look forward to working with each of you to bring this conference to life.

Toronto is a vibrant and dynamic city that is renowned for its cultural diversity and welcoming spirit. As such, it is the perfect setting for a conference that seeks to promote global understanding and cooperation. During your stay, I encourage you to explore the city, sample its delicious cuisine, and participate in exciting cultural events.

I am confident that CANMUN will be a transformative and unforgettable experience for all of you. I look forward to welcoming you to Toronto and to our conference.

Sincerely,

Sarah ChongXi Hu

Secretary General
