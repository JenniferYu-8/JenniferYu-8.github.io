The Dais

Meet Elisa Fiore, 16 year old a grade 11 student at Lorne Park Secondary School. Elisa is a director here at CANMUN!! Elisa has been part-taking in Model UN since grade 10! She regularly says that Model UN changed her highschool experiences, meeting countless friends and having so many new memories. In grade 11, Elisa was a part of the executive team for her school's MUN club, and in grade 12 will be the president. While this is her first time being a director, Elisa is proud to say she has attended and succeeded at over 10 conferences (2022-2023), planning on participating in many more! In addition to MUN, Elisa has been playing rep hockey since grade 5, was the captain of her school lacrosse team, and loves to wakeboard in the summer! Elisa is thrilled to see all the amazing delegates in action at CANMUN 2024!
